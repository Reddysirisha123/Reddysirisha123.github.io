Netflix User Data Analysis

A comprehensive analysis of Netflix subscriber data, revenue patterns, and user behavior.

Interactive Dashboard

Key Areas of Analysis

Demographic Analysis

Explores the age and gender distribution across different subscription types, helping to understand which demographic groups are most attracted to each plan.

Geographic Insights

Examines the distribution of users across different countries and analyzes country-specific metrics such as total revenue and user growth rates.

Subscription Patterns

Investigates the popularity of different subscription plans, average subscription duration, and trends in user sign-ups over time.

Revenue Analysis

Calculates monthly revenue by subscription type, total revenue by country, and Average Revenue Per User (ARPU) across different segments.

User Behavior

Analyzes payment patterns, device usage preferences, and the correlation between device types and subscription plans.

Retention and Churn

Examines user retention based on join dates and last payment dates, and analyzes churn rates across different user segments.

Growth and Trends

Looks at user base growth rates in different countries and overall sign-up trends over time.
